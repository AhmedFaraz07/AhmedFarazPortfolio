RAIDIO

Hack Without Borders 2026 • Hardware / Python
March 24-25, 2026

Overview

This project was engineered for the Hack Without Borders 2026 hackathon by me, Meher Patel, Tomas Chen, & Tayeb Marikar. The goal was to create a robust system capable of providing offline AI access utilizing analog radio protocols.

Inspiration

After doing research what we discovered was that as the entire world is revolutionized by AI and LLM Transformers, many in undeveloped, rural villages cannot access this technology due to a lack of pre-existing internet infrastructure. We wanted to fill this gap with a realistic solution, so to say, a starting step towards the right direction.
