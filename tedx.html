A Nuanced Dawn of Education

TEDx Talk
October 10, 2024

Overview

As part of the "Imagine the Impossible" TEDx event held at International Schools Group (ISG) Dammam, I had the honor of taking the stage to explore the rapidly evolving intersection of Artificial Intelligence and learning.

  • Developed a TEDx speech focusing on AI's role in education and how it is reshaping the way we process information.
  • Guided educational institutions on adapting to the rise of Large Language Models (LLMs), moving away from strict prohibition and towards responsible integration.
  • Represented my school on a large public stage, showcasing my insights on AI and technological advancement.
  • Enhanced public speaking and research skills through the rigorous preparation, drafting, and live delivery of the speech.

Press & Recognition

Following the event, I was honored to be interviewed by Arab News to discuss my perspectives on AI's integration into modern education systems and the future of tech-driven learning.
